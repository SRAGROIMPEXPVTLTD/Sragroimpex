About us

COMPANY PROFILE

Rice is by far one of the most important commercial food crops as it is considered a main source of nourishment for over half the world's population. Therefore, S.R. OVERSEAS was established in 2004 with the main purpose of manufacturing rice. We are a name one can trust to obtain varieties of rice such as Basmati Rice, 1121 Basmati Traditional Rice, Long Grain Rice, and many more. Our name comes first in satisfying customers' requirements when it comes to well-cleaned and sorted rice according to high standards of quality, hygienically processed and packed through the latest state-of-the-art technology to maintain the highest international standards. This has helped us become a top-notch manufacturer, exporter, and supplier in this industry.

MESSAGE FROM CEO

In 2004, Rajesh Gupta, fired with entrepreneurial zeal, started a rice unit in Karnal known as "S.R. OVERSEAS," situated near the foothills of the great Himalayas. Our vision is to be a global rice export company as well as establish a strong presence across India. Our vision is to make S.R. OVERSEAS a household brand conglomerate by providing an entire range of rice qualities for every section of consumers. Our mission is to deliver value to the customer and establish leadership in the core market, strive towards diversification and expansion of the product portfolio, focus on increasing global footprints by venturing into new markets, and forge fruitful alliances with entities operating in the same domain. Rajesh Gupta, being a role model, has woven his company into a strong team of professionals committed to the values of trust, honesty, commitment, and quality. These values guide us in dealing with our associates, vendors, and customers, and we believe they will help us remain at the forefront of the basmati export and domestic market.

MISSION & VISION

As one of the leading players in the rice industry, we are committed to delivering excellence and quality to our customers, cultivating a professional work environment that fosters employee growth and satisfaction, and building a financially strong, growth-oriented company that creates value for our stakeholders. Our mission is to perpetuate and upgrade the legacy of rice in India by leveraging world-class rice processing technologies, aiming to become the industry benchmark for product quality and customer service.

QUALITY POLICY

We believe that the only raw material needed for commercial production of rice is the rice seed or seedlings that we procure from different regions with an emphasis on quality. Satisfying the product-related needs of our clients successfully is our prime motive, and therefore, we leave no stone unturned to deliver them the desired quality. We are equipped with the world's most acceptable technologies of 'Buhler' & 'Satake'. With our vast experience in this field, we have gained specialization in understanding our clients' requirements and fulfilling them in a better manner. We adhere to every standard of food-grade quality, and as a result, we are certified with ISO 22000, HACCP & GMP.
