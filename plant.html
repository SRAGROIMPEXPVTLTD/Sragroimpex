Plant

Parboiling/Sella Unit

Firstly, the paddy is fed into parboiling unit for the making process of parboiled or steamed rice. S.R. OVERSEAS has the most innovative technology and skilled engineers to make the quality at best level. We utilize R.O treated water for paddy process, this adds to our plus point

Packing Unit

For the purpose of the packing, S.R OVERSEAS is installed with Automatic packing machine to gain the highest level of accuracy. We utilize NITROGEN filling in the pouches to assure the longer age of the product. We are capable to pack and handle the different sizes ranging from 1kg to 50kg. And we also customize the packing sizes according to buyers demand

Whitening/Polishing unit

To turn the grains from brown to white, we are equipped with world's most acceptable technology of Buhler, Germany. Our technicians are always remain active on quality check to process the grains with highest level of finish & low breakage

Warehousing

After packing, we store the goods in hygienic warehouses if any delay in loading occurs. To insure the life of the goods, we are subscribed to pest control service, which timely checks the health of the material.

SORTEX UNIT

To gain 100% sorted rice, S.R. OVERSEAS is equipped with colors sortex machine of Satake, Japan. Ensuring the quality at every angle always remains our prime motto
